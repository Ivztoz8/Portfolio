I was born in Bologna in 1987.
As for my childhood I will be brief, I had a turbulent one full of family problems.
The schools I attended after the normal basic routine were: a scientific high school, the faculty of mathematical sciences, an accounting and tourism technical institute (not finding work as a high school student / graduate, I tried to reinvent myself) and changing again.
Around the age of 25 I chose to enter the world of social studies and I attended the Faculty of Educational Sciences and, to date, while I continue to reinvent myself and make my passions become job opportunities, I am attending an online university for the Computer Engineering course and, in the meantime, in 2022, I attended the Master in Full Stack Web Developer of the Bollean Academy.

As far as my skills are concerned, I would like to represent them as a building and, I think that the foundations of this imaginary structure is the tireless desire to learn, study and learn that distinguishes me, not always, but from around the age of twenty to today.
On the first floor of this hypothetical building there are the study of technology and information technology and consequently the programming languages:
C/C++, Java, Python, R, Javascript, PHP, Ruby and the no less important markup and design languages HTML, CSS.
Also on this floor is a wing dedicated to the study of robotics, operating systems, computer networks and virtual machines.
On the second floor of the fortress of skills is the passion for finance and economics that I developed about four years ago.
At the center of this floor was built a statue of Warren Buffett who periodically my passion for finance honors with incense and accommodating interest rates. On the top floor, (yes I know it's a dwarf fortress or a very low building) there is the passion for computer graphics and therefore the ability to use programs such as Autocad, Archicad, Google Sketch-Up, Blender , Autodesk Maya 3D, Adobe Photoshop, Gimp, 3d Studio Max, etc.
